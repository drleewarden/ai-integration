## Seminar — run of show (90 min)
1. **Hook (5 min):** live automation demo on something every small biz hates (auto-draft a quote/invoice from an email; auto-reply to enquiries). Show, don't explain yet.
2. **Why now (10 min):** the shift, risk of doing nothing, you as the calm expert (cut hype).
3. **5 highest-ROI automations (30 min):** enquiry handling · quoting & docs · marketing content · booking & follow-ups · reporting. One **live build**.
4. **How to start (20 min):** framework — **Audit → Pick one bottleneck → Pilot → Scale.** Give a Monday-usable takeaway.
5. **The offer (10 min):** "Book a free 20-min Automation Opportunity Audit — QR on screen. First 5 tonight get a written 1-page automation plan." Time-boxed + limited.
6. **Q&A (15 min).**

**Convert in the room:** QR on final slide + a card on every seat + iPad/sign-up sheet at the back. Book before they leave.
